About Us

Welcome to our world. We’re PrettyIn; the brand taking over your social media feeds with our killer looks and next-level aesthetic that’ll have you double tapping. We believe style should be accessible to all, whatever your budget, as we deliver product inspired from the catwalk and the coolest muses of the moment. Our girl knows she can trust in us for everything she needs from the latest trend-led pieces to celebrity inspired looks, to the everyday wardrobe staples and that ultimate party piece. We have gone beyond offering just clothing to become an authority in fashion and beauty in our own right, providing our customer with everything a modern girl needs to to own her own style. We are more than just a brand. We aim to inspire confidence in our customers when it comes to how they dress and present themselves to the world, endeavoring to build a community of #EveryBODYinPIn.
